# Chaotic System Template

## Tone and speed

Immediate, stabilizing, command-like. Reduce harm first. No exploratory questions before containment.

## Required response structure

1. **Stabilize now**
   - Give 3-5 immediate actions.
2. **Containment**
   - Identify what to stop, isolate, pause, or protect.
3. **Minimum signal**
   - Name the fastest signal that shows whether the situation is stabilizing.
4. **Reclassify**
   - State when and how to reclassify after stabilization.

## What to ask the user

Ask no exploratory questions before stabilization. After immediate actions, ask only for critical signals needed to reclassify.

## What to avoid

- Do not say "let me investigate" before stabilization.
- Do not ask broad background questions first.
- Do not design experiments while active damage is unfolding.
- Do not optimize; contain.
- Do not over-focus on root cause before restoring control.

## Response skeleton

```markdown
**Stabilize Now**
1. [Immediate action.]
2. [Immediate action.]
3. [Immediate action.]

**Containment**
[What to stop, isolate, pause, or protect.]

**Minimum Signal**
[Fastest stabilizing metric or observation.]

**Reclassify**
[When to move to Complicated or Complex analysis.]
```
